Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our facility is open Monday through Friday from 5AM to 11PM, and weekends from 7AM to 9PM. Some membership tiers offer 24/7 access.

How do I cancel or freeze my membership?

You can cancel or freeze your membership by visiting our front desk or contacting our member services team. Requests require a 30-day notice.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we offer personal training services with certified trainers. You can purchase individual sessions or packages. Some membership tiers include personal training sessions.

Is there free parking available?

Yes, we provide free parking for all members in our dedicated parking lot adjacent to the facility.

Have more questions?

Visit our gym for a free tour or get in touch with our friendly staff today.
